Game_of_Thrones

George R.R. Martin's best-selling book series "A Song of Ice and Fire" is brought to the screen as HBO sinks its considerable storytelling teeth into the medieval fantasy epic. It's the depiction of two powerful families -- kings and queens, knights and renegades, liars and honest men -- playing a deadly game for control of the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros, and to sit atop the Iron Throne.

  • First episode date: April 17, 2011.
  • Theme song: Game of Thrones Theme.
  • Writers: George R. R. Martin, David Benioff, D.B. Weiss, Bryan Cogman, Jane Espenson, Vanessa Taylor, Dave Hill


Why I like Game of throne

It has an unpredictable story line that will make your heart beat at the speed of light. It's a mix of fantasy, action, adventure and suspense with the typical elements of drama and romance. Set in the middle/dark ages in an alternate universe, the story is about a bunch of strong families (known as 'houses') who want to ascend the Iron Throne (the throne on which the ruler of the seven kingdoms sit) Basically it's a story of cunning power play where everyone tries to one up each other. It's full of sly and strategic planning where you can trust no one. However, it doesn't stop to that. It has a healthy mix of fantasy elements like Dragons, magical creatures and hints of of magic to make it more interesting; with parallel story lines to enrich it. Visit here for more information



My favourite Three actor/actress in Game Of Thrones

Alfie Allen

I could write a big rant about him, because he is one of the best actors out there and totally underrated just because his role hasn’t gained that much attention and liking! Theon Greyjoy had a character development like no one else. He started off as a cocky boy, transformed into an emotional wrack and into Reek, back to an entirely new personality.

Emilia Clarke

-Like Daenerys or not, you can’t deny that Emilia seems like a fantastic person in real life. Daenerys doesn’t show a lot of emotions - and I understand the critism aimed at her character/acting, but Emilia is totally different (at least in interviews and at appearances) Just go to Youtube and search up some of her interviews: they are great and she’s so hilarious.

Peter Dinklage

Tyrion is one of the few characters left to root for, and in his performance, Peter Dinklage proves that it was the role he was born to play. Dinklage’s performance is bigger compared to some of the others on the show, and that totally works for his character. As the series goes on, Tyrion faces more and more physical and internal battles, which shows off Dinklage’s range more than it did in earlier seasons



My best Quote from Game of Thrones


“Never forget what you are, for surely the world will not. Make it your strength. Then it can never be your weakness. Armour yourself in it, and it will never be used to hurt you.” George R.R. Martin, A Game of Thrones



Budget


As the show has gotten more and more ambitious, the budget has reflected that shift as well. During season 2, the average budget per episode was $6 million USD. For season 6, that went all the way up to $10 million USD — making the entire season’s production budget $100 million USD.
